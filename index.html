About Me

I am a data analyst with a strong foundation in industrial engineering and harnessing data to drive business impact since 2017 in international companies. My expertise lies in transforming raw data into actionable insights, with a focus on reporting, KPI management, and process optimization. I have a proven track record of enhancing system reliability, report accuracy, and operational efficiency. Data Exploration for Global Superstore Dataset using SQL Server

This project demonstrates SQL skills in data exploration and analysis using the Walmart Global Superstore dataset. The analysis focuses on deriving insights from sales, profit, customer segmentation, and seasonal trends across regions, especially in the United States.
This exploration provides insights into profitable products, customer behavior by segment, and shipping costs, offering a foundation for strategic business decisions. Skills utilized include data cleaning, aggregation, cumulative analysis, and trend identification.
